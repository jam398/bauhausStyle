## ğŸ“š Quick Reference

### Bauhaus Core Principles
1. **Form follows function** - Design serves purpose
2. **Unity of art & craft** - No hierarchy between disciplines
3. **Geometric abstraction** - Pure forms (circle, square, triangle)
4. **Primary colors + neutrals** - Red, blue, yellow, black, white
5. **Grid-based composition** - Mathematical structure
6. **Experimental materials** - Embrace new technology
7. **Mass production** - Design for industrial age
8. **Teaching through making** - Learning by doing

### Neo-Modernism Key Differences
- **Responsive & fluid** vs. fixed Bauhaus layouts
- **Digital-native** materials (screens, motion, interaction)
- **Accessibility-focused** (WCAG, universal design)
- **Performance-optimized** (web standards, speed)
- **Data-driven** typography & spacing systems
- **Component-based** architecture (design systems)
- **Open-source** ethos vs. craft guild model
